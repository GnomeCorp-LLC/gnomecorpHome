        Also known as GLEGGSD, this branch is responsible for all dedicated software development, gaming related activities, and
        software debugging

#### GnomeCorp. Interstellar Travel, Technologies, & Management Agency - (GITTMA)
        Manages all interstellar travel and internal super-starways for shipping travel and other various commerical reasons. Also handles various extra-galactic foreign affairs, and megaprojects, such as the System E36B0-1 DYSON-MEGASPHERE's saftey, if any faults or dangers are found, the star system gets fined and they need to fix it.

#### GnomeCorp. Civillian & Municipal Department - (GCCMD)
        Handles all civillian and municipal needs for planetary settlements, does not handle starships, deep sea research facilities, or deep-space voyagers.

#### Gnome Experimental Research & Development Program - (GERDP)
        A much more recently developed branch, GERDP focuses on developing and discovering new technologies, such as the newest discovery in wormhole technologies, the VcWHG-M, the smallest, and possibly commerically avalible, Void Portal capable wormhole generator to be tested! 

        Then, when GERDP deems a technology fit (along with checks from other branches), the technology gets sent off to another branch, such as GnomeTech, for further technological improvements and manufacturing to be developed.

#### GnomeCorp. Digital Communications & High Powered Transmissions Department - (GDHPTD)
        Sometimes seen as GDCHPTD, this branch manages all over air digital communication (GnomeRadio, GnomeWork Connected, etc.) as well as (in combination with GITTMA), managing all interstellar HyperLight-1 to HyperLight-6.4 communication networks, as well as ProbeNet, DSPCN (Deep Space Probe Connection Network), and the Emergency Municipal & Deep Space Emergency Notification Program (EMDSE)

#### Gnome-O-Nautics air, land, and sea travel - (GoNalst)
